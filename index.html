Dr. Israr Ahmed

Israr Ahmed (1932–2010) was a distinguished Islamic scholar and revivalist from Pakistan.

He devoted his life to spreading the message of the Qur’an and advocating for Islamic reform and unity.

  • Born: April 26, 1932, in Hisar, British India.
  • Migration: Migrated to Pakistan after the Partition in 1947.
  • Education: Graduated from King Edward Medical College, Lahore (MBBS).
  • Career Shift: Left medical practice to dedicate his life to Islamic scholarship and revival.
  • Founded: Established Tanzeem-e-Islami in 1975.
  • Known For: Powerful Qur’anic lectures and advocacy for Islamic reform and Khilafah.
  • Died: April 14, 2010, in Lahore, Pakistan.
